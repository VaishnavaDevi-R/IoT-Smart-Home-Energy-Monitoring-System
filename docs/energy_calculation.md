# Energy Calculation

## Power Calculation

Power represents the instantaneous electrical load.

Formula:

P = V × I

Where:

* P = Power (Watts)
* V = Voltage (Volts)
* I = Current (Amperes)

Example:

230V × 5A = 1150W

---

## Energy Calculation

Energy represents electricity consumed over time.

Formula:

Energy = Power × Time

Example:

1150W for 2 hours

Energy = 2.3 kWh

---

## Cost Calculation

Formula:

Cost = Energy × Tariff

Assuming:

Tariff = ₹8 per kWh

Example:

2.3 × 8 = ₹18.40

---

## Alert Thresholds

Normal:

Less than 1000W

Medium Usage:

1000W – 1999W

High Usage:

2000W and above
