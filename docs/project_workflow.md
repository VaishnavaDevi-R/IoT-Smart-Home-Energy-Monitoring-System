# Project Workflow

## System Overview

The Smart Home Energy Monitoring System simulates and monitors household energy consumption using appliance-based energy profiles.

---

## Workflow

Smart Appliances
↓
Power Consumption Simulation
↓
Energy Calculation
↓
Cost Estimation
↓
Alert Generation
↓
CSV Logging
↓
ThingSpeak Cloud Upload
↓
Graph Generation
↓
PDF Report Generation
↓
Streamlit Dashboard

---

## Components

### Appliance Simulator

Simulates realistic home appliance usage based on time of day.

### Energy Calculator

Calculates power and energy consumption.

### Cost Estimator

Estimates electricity cost based on tariff rates.

### Alert Engine

Detects high energy consumption conditions.

### ThingSpeak Integration

Uploads live data to the cloud.

### Report Generator

Stores energy data and generates reports.

### Streamlit Dashboard

Provides real-time analytics and visualization.
